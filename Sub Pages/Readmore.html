Maharashtrian Wedding

This is the official engagement ceremony or the Roka ceremony in Marathi/Maharashtrian wedding, which is signified by Sakhar or sweets. What should you say at a wedding? To the man: "I, (his name), take you (her name ), to be my wife, to have and to hold from this day forward, for better, for worse, for richer, for poorer, in sickness and in health, to love and to cherish, till death do us part."
